# AI & Machine Learning

Patterns: 1

## 1. PDF-to-Image Structural Synchronization

Automating the pairing of legacy report photos (PDF) with raw site photos (JPG) by matching persistent visual landmarks like background buildings or road markings.

### Steps

1. Extract raw images from legacy PDF documents while preserving page-to-image mapping
2. Perform visual feature comparison targeting structures that remain constant across different construction phases
3. Automate the creation of 'before/after' pairings to drive downstream folder organization and automated PDF report generation
