## 1. Construction Workflow Parallelism

Experienced practitioners recognize that construction phases are not sequential by folder number but run in parallel to meet regulatory and planning deadlines.

### Steps

1. Folder-based numbering (e.g., 01, 02, 05) often hides the fact that Contract, Permits, and Plans must start simultaneously to meet the project timeline.
2. Identify 'Phase 1: Preparation' as a parallel block where Permit applications (e.g., Road Usage) must be prioritized as external bottlenecks that can delay the entire project.
3. The '1st Owner Meeting' is a critical dependency node that requires prior completion of design review results and permit submissions to be productive.
